# Artificial Intelligence

**Created**: 2026-04-13  
**Updated**: 2026-04-13  
**Tags**: ai, machine learning, computer science, automation, intelligence

## Summary
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. It encompasses the development of algorithms and systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human cognition, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.

## Key Points
- AI is broadly categorized into Narrow AI (task-specific) and General AI (human-level reasoning across domains)
- Machine learning is a core subfield where systems learn from data without explicit programming
- Deep learning uses multi-layered neural networks to model complex patterns
- AI applications include natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and recommendation systems
- Modern AI is powered by large-scale compute, massive datasets, and transformer-based architectures
